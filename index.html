About Me

I'm a robotics software engineer based in Lübeck, Germany, with a strong interest in developing autonomous systems capable of operating reliably in complex environments.

My work focuses on SLAM, state estimation, sensor fusion and perception using LiDAR, radar and camera-based systems.

I also enjoy developing mobile robot platforms, working with embedded hardware, exploring open-source robotics tools, and keeping up with new research in autonomous navigation.
